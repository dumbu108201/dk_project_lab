Meetings are held with the higher hierarchy in the team to explain the scope of the project and talk about the deliverables and costs. Project drafts are prepared by the Project manager and conversations with client are held to explain the brief model and features of the car. Any changes that the client suggests are occurred in this phase.  The location for manufacturing and assembly is decided during this phase. The knowledge transfer from the Project Manager to the team on what the client’s requirements are and how to accomplish this within the given budget and time is completed. Any inputs form the team members or experience on previous similar projects that are worked on are discussed.
